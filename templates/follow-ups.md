# Follow-ups

Templates for post-purchase check-ins, re-engagement, and keeping in touch.

---

### 1. Post-Purchase Check In (1 week)
```
Hi {{name}},

It's been about a week since you got your {{product_or_service}}. How's everything going?

If you've got any questions or need help with anything, just reply here. We're always happy to help.
```

### 2. Review Request
```
Hi {{name}} 👋

Hope you're enjoying your {{product_or_service}}!

Would you mind leaving us a quick review? It really helps other people find us.

{{review_link}}

Thanks so much! 🙏
```

### 3. Re-engagement (Inactive Customer)
```
Hey {{name}}, long time no chat! 👋

Just checking in — we've got some new {{products_or_services}} that might interest you.

Take a look: {{link}}

If you'd like to stop getting messages from us, just reply STOP.
```

### 4. Subscription Renewal Reminder
```
Hi {{name}},

Your {{subscription_name}} is up for renewal on {{date}}.

Want to continue? No action needed — it'll renew automatically.

Want to make changes or cancel? Just reply and we'll sort it out.
```

### 5. Cross-sell Suggestion
```
Hi {{name}},

Since you got {{original_purchase}}, we thought you might also like:

{{suggestion_1}} — {{brief_desc}}
{{suggestion_2}} — {{brief_desc}}

Interested? Just reply and we'll send more details.
```

### 6. Birthday / Anniversary
```
Happy birthday, {{name}}! 🎂

Here's a little something from us — {{discount_or_offer}}.

Use code: {{code}}
Valid until: {{expiry}}

Enjoy your day!
```

### 7. Win-Back (Lost Customer)
```
Hey {{name}},

We noticed it's been a while since your last {{service_or_purchase}}. Everything OK?

We'd love to have you back. Here's {{offer}} to sweeten the deal.

No pressure — just wanted you to know we're still here if you need us.
```

### 8. Feedback Request
```
Hi {{name}},

We're always trying to get better. Got 2 minutes to answer a quick question?

How would you rate your experience with {{business_name}} out of 10?

Just reply with a number. Honest answers only — we can take it! 😄
```
