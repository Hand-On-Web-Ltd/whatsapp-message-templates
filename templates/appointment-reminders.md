# Appointment Reminders

Templates for booking confirmations, reminders, and rescheduling.

---

### 1. Booking Confirmation
```
Confirmed! ✅

Hi {{name}}, your appointment is booked:

📅 {{date}}
⏰ {{time}}
📍 {{location}}

Need to change anything? Just reply to this message.
```

### 2. 24-Hour Reminder
```
Quick reminder, {{name}} 👋

Your appointment is tomorrow:

📅 {{date}} at {{time}}
📍 {{location}}

Reply YES to confirm or let us know if you need to reschedule.
```

### 3. Same-Day Reminder
```
See you today, {{name}}! 

Your appointment is at {{time}}.

📍 {{location}}
🅿️ {{parking_info}}

Running late? No worries — just give us a heads up.
```

### 4. Reschedule Request
```
Hi {{name}},

Unfortunately we need to move your appointment on {{original_date}}.

Here are some alternative times:
• {{option_1}}
• {{option_2}}
• {{option_3}}

Just reply with which works best for you. Sorry for the hassle!
```

### 5. No-Show Follow Up
```
Hi {{name}},

We missed you at your appointment today. No worries — things come up!

Want to rebook? Just reply with a date and time that works for you, and we'll get it sorted.
```

### 6. Recurring Appointment Reminder
```
Hi {{name}},

Just a heads up — your regular {{service}} is coming up:

📅 {{date}} at {{time}}

Same time, same place. Reply SKIP if you want to miss this one, or CHANGE to pick a different time.
```

### 7. Post-Appointment Follow Up
```
Hi {{name}},

Hope everything went well with your {{service}} today!

If you've got any questions or need anything else, just message us here.

Thanks for choosing {{business_name}} 🙏
```
