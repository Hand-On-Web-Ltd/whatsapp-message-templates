# Welcome Messages

Templates for greeting new customers or leads on WhatsApp.

---

### 1. Simple Welcome
```
Hi {{name}} 👋

Thanks for getting in touch with {{business_name}}! We got your message and we'll get back to you shortly.

In the meantime, check out our website: {{website}}

Talk soon!
```

### 2. Service Enquiry Response
```
Hi {{name}},

Thanks for asking about our {{service_name}}. Here's what you need to know:

📋 What's included: {{brief_description}}
💰 Starting from: {{price}}
⏱️ Turnaround: {{timeframe}}

Want to book a quick call to go over the details? Just reply with a time that works for you.
```

### 3. New Customer Onboarding
```
Welcome to {{business_name}}, {{name}}! 🎉

Here are a few things to get you started:

1️⃣ {{step_one}}
2️⃣ {{step_two}}
3️⃣ {{step_three}}

Got questions? Just message us here — we check WhatsApp throughout the day.
```

### 4. After Website Form Submission
```
Hey {{name}},

We just got your form from our website — thanks for filling that in.

A member of our team will be in touch within {{response_time}} to discuss next steps.

If it's urgent, give us a call on {{phone}}.
```

### 5. Referral Welcome
```
Hi {{name}} 👋

{{referrer_name}} mentioned you might be interested in {{service}}. Great to hear!

I'm {{your_name}} from {{business_name}}. Happy to chat through what we can do for you.

When's a good time for a quick call?
```

### 6. Event Registration Confirmation
```
You're in, {{name}}! ✅

Here are the details for {{event_name}}:

📅 {{date}}
⏰ {{time}}
📍 {{location_or_link}}

We'll send you a reminder the day before. See you there!
```

### 7. After-Hours Auto Reply
```
Hi there! 👋

Thanks for your message. We're currently closed but we'll get back to you first thing tomorrow morning.

Our hours: {{business_hours}}

If it's urgent: {{emergency_contact}}
```
