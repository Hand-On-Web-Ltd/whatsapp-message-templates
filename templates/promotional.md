# Promotional Messages

Templates for sales, offers, and announcements. Remember: always include an opt-out option.

---

### 1. Flash Sale
```
⚡ Flash Sale — {{discount}}% off everything!

Hi {{name}}, for the next {{duration}} only:

🛒 {{offer_details}}
🔗 {{link}}

Don't hang about — it ends {{end_time}}.

Reply STOP to opt out of promotions.
```

### 2. New Product/Service Launch
```
Something new from {{business_name}} 🆕

Hi {{name}}, we just launched {{product_or_service}}!

Here's what it does:
✅ {{benefit_1}}
✅ {{benefit_2}}
✅ {{benefit_3}}

Check it out: {{link}}

Reply STOP to unsubscribe.
```

### 3. Seasonal Offer
```
{{season}} offer for you, {{name}} 🎄

{{offer_description}}

📅 Valid: {{start_date}} - {{end_date}}
💰 Save: {{amount_or_percentage}}
🔗 {{link}}

Don't miss out!

Reply STOP to opt out.
```
