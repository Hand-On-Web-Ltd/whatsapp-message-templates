## How to Use

1. Pick a template
2. Replace the `{{placeholders}}` with your actual details
3. Paste into WhatsApp Business or your messaging platform
4. Send it

## Tips

- Keep messages short — people skim on WhatsApp
- Use the customer's first name when you can
- Don't send promos more than once a week
- Always include an opt-out option for marketing messages
- Emojis are fine, but don't overdo it

## Works With

- WhatsApp Business App
- WhatsApp Business API (Twilio, MessageBird, etc.)
- Any platform that sends WhatsApp messages
